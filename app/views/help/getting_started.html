Joining a Class

To join a course you need a Course Enrollment key from your instructor. This key will be in the form ABCDE-12345. Once you have your key you can join the course. If you donâ€™t have a key please contact your instructor.

  1. Choose "Join a new course" from the "Courses" menu.
  2. Enter the course Enrollment Key and click "Enroll".
  3. You can now go to the course from the "Courses" menu.

What is Due Next?

To see what is due next, click on "Dashboard", this will give you an overview of all your courses, recent announcements, and when assignments are due in each course.

Questions, Notes, and Confused

    Questions

    To ask a question during a lecture, click on the question button at teh bottom of the video player (or type "q" on the keyboard). You can choose whether to make a question public or private. Public questions are visible to other students in the class so they can vote them or reply to them.

    • You can always delete a question by clicking on the X next to the question
    • You can reply to other students' questions by clicking on reply and typing your response
    • You can vote to have the teacher answer other students' questions in-class by clicking on the up arrow next to the question.
    • If you find an inappropriate or offensive question or reply, please click on the flag to inform the teacher

    Notes

    To take a note during a lecture, click on the note button at the bottom of the video player (or type "n" on the keyboard). When you're done with your note, type return to save it. You can go back to the place in the lecture with the note by clicking on it.

    • You can export your notes to your own computer or to print them from the settings menu

    Confused

    If you're confused during a lecture, you can click on the confused button at the bottom of the video player (or type "c" on the keyboard) to let the teacher know.

Reviewing My Progress

    To see your progress in a class, click on the course menu in the upper left of the screen. A green check indicates that you have completed a module or lecture on time.

Contacting My Teacher

    To contact your teacher, choose the course from the course menu and click on the teacher to send an email.

Technical Problems or Feedback

    If you have technical problems or feedback about ScalableLearning, please choose "Feedback" from the "Help" menu and we'll get back to you as quickly as we can. We truly appreciate your comments and feedback.

Privacy

    ScalableLearning takes your privacy seriously and we encourage you to read our privacy policy under the "Help" menu.
