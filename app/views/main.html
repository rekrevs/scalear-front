Active Learning in the Classroom and Online

ScalableLearning helps teachers combine the best of online student preparation and in-class teaching for active learning and flipped classroom teaching.

Student Preparation

Get students ready for class with interactive online lectures, in-video quizzes, surveys, voting, and student-led discussions.

Teacher Analysis

Understand where your students need help in the classroom and connect with their preparation through analytics and online discussions.

In-class Learning

Bring online questions and quizzes directly into the classroom to focus in-class teaching on your students’ needs.

Getting Started

With ScalableLearning teachers easily add quizzes and surveys to videos, understand where their students need help, and bring the students’ questions into the classroom for student-focused teaching.

Getting Started for Teachers

Learn how to create a course with interactive video quizzes, review your students’ performance and answer their questions, and choose material for use in-class.

Getting Started for Students

Learn how to work with interactive online lectures, ask questions, take notes, and tips for how to learn the most with ScalableLearning.

Use Cases

Flipped Classroom

ScalableLearning provides interactive online lectures to free in-class time for active teaching. ScalableLearning turns passive online videos into active learning experiences through in-video quizzes and discussions.

ScalableLearning shows teachers where students are struggling and enables them to easily bring online material for in-class review. With interactive online preparation, student analytics, and in-class review support, ScalableLearning is the ideal tool for flipped classroom teaching.

Distance Education

ScalableLearning turns online lectures into an active, social learning experience. Students can interact with the material, each other, and their teacher through online discussions, in-video quizzes, and voting.

Teachers receive feedback on how well students understood the material and can respond to questions and discussions right in the video. By bringing interactivity and social interaction to online videos, ScalableLearning is a powerful tool for enhancing distance education.
