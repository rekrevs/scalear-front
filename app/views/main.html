Active Learning in the Classroom and Online

ScalableLearning helps teachers combine the best of online interactive student preparation and active in-class teaching.

Student Preparation

Help students prepare for class with interactive online lectures, in-video quizzes, surveys, voting, and in-video student discussions and questions.

Teacher Analysis

Understand where your students need help in the classroom and connect with them through analytics and online discussions..

In-class Learning

Bring students’ online questions and quizzes directly into the classroom for review and use mobile devices in-class to engage student through Peer Instruction and voting.

Getting Started

With ScalableLearning teachers can easily add quizzes and surveys to videos for active student preparation, understand where students need help with teacher analytics, bring students’ online questions directly into the classroom, and use active in-class teaching techniques connected directly to the students’ online performance.

Use Cases

Flipped Classroom

ScalableLearning delivers interactive online videos to prepare students for active learning in-class. With ScalableLearning, your students can answer quizzes and surveys, ask questions, start discussions, and vote during online videos.

ScalableLearning shows teachers where students are struggling and makes it easy for them to bring this material into the classroom for review. ScalableLearning supports active learning in-class through student voting on mobile devices and after-class student follow-up of the in-class material.

With interactive online preparation, analytics for teachers, focused in-class review, active in-class student voting, and after-class student review, ScalableLearning is the ideal tool for active/flipped classroom teaching.

Distance Education

ScalableLearning turns online lectures into active, social learning experiences. Students can interact with the material, ask questions, and discuss with each other and their teachers through in-video quizzes, discussions, and voting.

For distance courses, ScalableLearning offers online Peer Instruction, where pairs of students can work together remotely in a synchronized manner to explore the material and help each other build understanding.

Teachers receive feedback on how well students understood the material and can respond to questions and discussions right in the video. By bringing interactivity and social interaction to online material, ScalableLearning is a powerful tool for enhancing distance education.

ScalableLearning delivers fully active preparation and learning online and in-class.
